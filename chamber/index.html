Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote and help people wanting to work with the Great Plains Chamber of Commerce. The site should allow others to see all the companies working with Great Plains and it should help them get access to these companies. It should let them be aware of any deals or promotions are available to them upon joining the Chamber of Commerce.
