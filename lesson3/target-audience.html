Target Audience

My target audience includes anyone who is trying to start a buisness or already owns a buisness. Most of our audience will be older than 25, so we need to take that into account. Site should be easy to use and clearly states the benefits of the Chamber of commerce.

Persona One

This is Joseph, he is an established buisness owner and runs a bike shop here in Aberdeen with his father. His dad had great success, but they are both looking for more ways to connect with other buisnesses

Persona Two

This is Brandon. He just barley started a bakery with his wife and they are just trying to get started. The site should be optimized for new couples so that they can easily see how this will help them without too much buisness lingo.

Senario One

Buisness majors graduating college wanting to kickstart there buisness.

Senario Two

Long term established buisnesses not too familiar with the internet.
